      Startup Guide & Status Overview

Turn on the expansion board and wait for approximately 1 minute. When LED1
on the expansion board lights up blue and the buzzer emits a "beep" sound, it
indicates that the ROS configuration is complete and the device has
successfully started.
Once the robot has fully powered on, LED2 will blink once every second, and a
Wi-Fi hotspot prefixed with “HW” will be broadcast.
The default network mode is AP (Access Point) direct connection. When
connecting via the mobile app or remote desktop, use the password: hiwonder.
