                   MentorPi Introduction
1. Product Introduction
MentorPi is a ROS2 robot powered by the Raspberry Pi 5. It comes in two
variants: MentorPi-M1, which features a Mecanum-wheel chassis, and
MentorPi-A1, equipped with an Ackermann chassis. Both models are built with
high-performance hardware, including a TOF Lidar and a 3D depth camera,
enabling advanced functionalities such as mapping, navigation, path planning,
image recognition, vision tracking, and human-robot interaction.
